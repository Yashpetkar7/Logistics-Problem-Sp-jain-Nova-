RFID FIRST

Your card is the fastest lane.

A chip sticker turns the existing student card into a tap card — tap the reader at the door and you're aboard, phone optional. Any USB reader works, plug-and-play. Lost the card? Block it from your pass in one tap and every bus rejects it instantly while your QR keeps working.

Why nobody sneaks past it

Six guardrails, layered. Cheating becomes harder than paying.

🔄Rotating codesThe QR re-generates every 30 seconds — screenshots die before they reach a friend.
🪪Photo on every scanThe owner's photo flashes to the driver. A borrowed pass shows the wrong face.
🚫Anti-passbackOne green per student per trip. The phone passed back through the window scans red.
🔐Card kill switchLost RFID card? Blocked in one message — every bus rejects it instantly.
🛡️Hardened backendHashed PINs, expiring sessions, rate-limited logins, device-keyed validators, audit trail.
🧾Everything loggedEvery green and red lands in the console live, with a reason. Enforcement moves to the office.
NOT JUST A TURNSTILE

Every tap is a logistics decision.

Boardings stream into a demand heatmap the moment they happen. Nova turns them into fleet recommendations — add a bus at the 8:30 rush, stretch the quiet 2pm run — so the shuttle costs less and waits shorter.
