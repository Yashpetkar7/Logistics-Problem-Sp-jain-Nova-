Why nobody sneaks past it

Five guardrails, layered. Cheating becomes harder than paying.

🔄Rotating codesThe QR re-generates every 30 seconds — screenshots die before they reach a friend.
🪪Photo on every scanThe owner's photo flashes to the driver. A borrowed pass shows the wrong face.
🚫Anti-passbackOne green per student per trip. The phone passed back through the window scans red.
📡Works offlineSignature maths needs no internet — the bus verifies riders anywhere on the route.
🧾Everything loggedEvery green and red lands in the console with a reason. Enforcement happens in the office, not at the door.
💳Spare seats earnNon-residents buy single, day, or weekly passes in two taps — freeloaders become customers.
Not just a turnstile

Every tap is a logistics decision.

Boardings tell you exactly when demand peaks and where seats run empty. Nova turns that into a fleet recommendation — add a bus at the 8:30 rush, stretch the quiet 2pm run — so the shuttle costs less and waits shorter.
